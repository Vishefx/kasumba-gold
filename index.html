Who We Are

Trusted Gold
From the Source

Kasumba Gold is a licensed gold trading and export business headquartered in Kampala, Uganda. We operate under full authorisation from the Ministry of Minerals and Energy of Uganda, ensuring every transaction meets legal and international standards.

We source premium natural alluvial gold directly from trusted miners in the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC) — one of the world's richest gold-producing regions. Our established supply chain guarantees consistent quality, reliable volumes, and complete traceability.

We work with refineries, bullion dealers, importers, and private investors worldwide. Every shipment is fully documented, assayed, and exported through official Ugandan customs channels.

What We Do

Our Services

01
⛏️
Gold Procurement

We source high-grade alluvial and hard-rock gold directly from vetted miners in eastern DRC, guaranteeing consistent quality and traceable supply for every order.

02
🔬
Assay & Certification

All gold is independently tested and certified for purity — typically 22K to 24K. Full assay reports are provided with every shipment for buyer verification.

03
📦
Export & Shipping

We manage the full export process from Kampala — URA customs clearance, Ministry export permits, and insured international courier delivery.

04
📄

Every deal includes Ministry license copies, export permits, assay certificate, ICGLR compliance records, and full invoice — meeting international due diligence.

05

We accommodate orders from 500g trial shipments to multi-kilogram monthly supply contracts, with pricing pegged to the LME spot rate.

06

Serious buyers are welcome to visit our Kampala office to inspect gold, review original documents, and meet our team before committing.
